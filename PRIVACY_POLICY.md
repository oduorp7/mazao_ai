# Privacy Policy — Mazao AI
Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Mazao AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019**.

## 1. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:
- **Telegram Identification**: Your Telegram ID and username to maintain your account.
- **Business Data**: Your business name and M-Pesa Till/Paybill number (if provided).
- **Individual Data**: Your name, KRA PIN (for labeling purposes), and employment status.
- **Transaction History**: Data extracted from M-Pesa statements (CSV/PDF) or SMS messages you explicitly share with the bot.

## 2. Why We Collect Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:
- **Compliance Tracking**: Generating reminders for KRA, SHA, and NSSF deadlines.
- **Financial Awareness**: Reconciling M-Pesa transactions to show profit/loss trends.
- **Utility Prediction**: Estimating when your electricity tokens or gas will run out.
- **Daily Reporting**: Sending you automated summaries of your business health.

## 3. Data Protection & Security

- **Encryption**: All data is stored in encrypted databases (Supabase) with Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring that your data is never accessible to other users.
- **Third-Party Sharing**: We do **not** sell your data. We share only necessary anonymised transaction data with AI models (Anthropic) for the purpose of generating your financial reports.

## 4. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Ask us to update or fix inaccurate information.
- **Deletion**: Request that we delete your entire data history by typing the `/delete_my_data` command (in development) or contacting us.
